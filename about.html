Our Mission

We believe software development should be collaborative, transparent, and efficient. We build tools that empower agile teams to do their best work.

Who We Are

TaskManager was founded by a team of software engineers, project managers, and designers who experienced firsthand the chaos of managing complex software delivery using disjointed tools and spreadsheets.

Since our launch in 2026, we have grown from a small open-source startup into a comprehensive SaaS solution adopted by hundreds of engineering teams globally. Built deeply around the principles of Agile methodology, TaskManager prioritizes flexibility without sacrificing control.

Core Values

Simplicity

Powerful features shouldn't require complex interfaces. We focus on clean, intuitive user experiences.

Transparency

Work is better when everyone shares the same context. Our systems are built to break down information silos.

Security

We support granular, role-based access to safeguard your proprietary data across thousands of tasks.

Evolution

We ship updates frequently. We listen to our users and adapt our platform to match new paradigms of work.
